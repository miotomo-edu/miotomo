# 🧬 MIOTOMO CHARACTER SYSTEM
## Floating Alien Design Specification (v6.0)

---

## 🧠 1. CORE PHILOSOPHY

> The character is not a body.  
> It is a **floating system of expressive objects**.

### Principles:
- No traditional anatomy
- Components are **visually aligned, not physically connected**
- Expression is driven by:
  - Eyes
  - Mouth block
  - Motion (tail)

---

## 🧱 2. CHARACTER STRUCTURE

### Vertical Stack (Top → Bottom)

1. Eyes  
2. Head  
3. Mouth block (front-mounted)  
4. Body (floating sphere)  
5. Tail (primary motion system)  

---

## 👀 3. EYES

### Form:
- Two large glossy spheres
- Black pupils
- White eyeballs

### Eyelids:
- Same color as **head (IMPORTANT)**
- Soft half-sphere base

### Rules:
- Eyes slightly touch or are very close
- Slight forward protrusion
- No eyebrows

### Function:
- Direction = attention
- Openness = emotion intensity

---

## 🧠 4. HEAD

### Shape:
- Vertical capsule / pill
- **Wider at the bottom**

### Material:
- Matte clay
- Soft shading
- Subtle micro texture

### Role:
- Holds eyes and mouth
- Defines silhouette identity

---

## 👄 5. MOUTH BLOCK

### Placement:
- Front-mounted on lower part of head
- NOT between head and body

### Shape:
- Rounded horizontal capsule
- Slight thickness (not flat)

### Size:
- Smaller than head width
- Fully contained inside head silhouette

### Position:
- Slightly above bottom edge of head
- Visible head surface below

### Function:
- Independent expression unit
- Changes shape, not position

---

## 🔵 6. BODY

### Shape:
- Perfect or near-perfect sphere

### Placement:
- Floating below head
- Medium gap between head and body

### Function:
- Visual balance
- Neutral mass

---

## 🖐️ 7. HANDS

### Form:
- Small rounded blobs (mitten-like)
- No fingers
- No arms

### Color:
- **Single solid pink color (NO gradient)**

### Placement:
- Floating beside body
- Slightly below body center

### Rules:
- Always visible (avoid missing limbs)
- Symmetrical positioning unless expressive

---

## 🌀 8. TAIL (PRIMARY MOTION SYSTEM)

### Type:
👉 **Whip Spiral Tail (ONLY variant used)**

### Shape:
- Spiral / coil
- Thick at top → thin at tip
- Smooth rounded geometry

### Color:
- **Same as feet tone (yellow)**

### Behavior:
- Always slightly delayed motion (follow-through)
- Primary driver of animation personality

---

### Tail Motion Examples:

| State        | Motion Description |
|--------------|------------------|
| Idle         | Gentle sway |
| Happy        | Soft bounce |
| Excited      | Fast oscillation |
| Thinking     | Tight coil |
| Curious      | Slight tilt |
| Angry        | Sharp whip snap |
| Playful      | Loop + flick |
| Sad          | Droop |

---

## 🌀 9. SPACING SYSTEM

### Required Gaps:

| Area           | Gap Size |
|----------------|--------|
| Eyes → Head    | Minimal |
| Head → Body    | Medium |
| Body → Tail    | Connected |
| Tail → Ground  | Floating |

### Rule:
> Components **must never visually merge**

---

## 🎨 10. COLOR SYSTEM (LOCKED)

### Palette:

| Element | Color | Hex |
|--------|------|-----|
| Head | Warm peach | #FFB07A |
| Mouth | Slightly darker peach | #FFD1A6 |
| Body | Muted teal | #B7E0D6 |
| Hands | Solid pink | #FFB7C8 |
| Tail | Soft yellow | #FFD46A |
| Eyes (white) | White | #FFFFFF |
| Eyes (pupil) | Black | #111111 |

---

### Color Rules:

- Head = always warm
- Body = always cool
- Mouth = same family as head
- Hands = **one flat pink (no gradient)**
- Tail = matches feet tone (yellow)
- No additional colors

---

## 🧴 11. MATERIAL & RENDER

### Material:
- Soft matte clay
- Slight micro-noise
- No gloss (except eyes)

### Eyes:
- Slightly glossy

### Lighting:
- Soft studio lighting
- Top-front light
- Very soft shadows

---

## 🎭 12. EXPRESSION SYSTEM

### Controlled ONLY by:

#### Eyes:
- Direction
- Size (slight scaling)
- Openness

#### Mouth:
- Curve (smile / flat / frown)
- Shape (open / closed / oval)

---

### ❌ Do NOT use:
- Eyebrows
- Extra facial features
- Texture changes for emotion

---

## ⚙️ 13. PROPORTIONS

| Element | Ratio |
|--------|------|
| Head | 45–50% |
| Body | 20–25% |
| Tail | 30–40% |
| Eyes | Large dominant |

---

## 🧬 14. SILHOUETTE RULES

### Key Shape Language:
- Strong rounded forms
- Clear separation between components

### Recognition Rules:
- Big head + big eyes
- Floating sphere body
- Distinct tail shape

### Must work in:
- Black silhouette
- Small sizes
- No color context

---

## 🚨 15. NON-NEGOTIABLE RULES

1. No arms  
2. No legs  
3. Mouth is on front of head  
4. Head is wider at bottom  
5. Hands are solid pink (no gradient)  
6. Eyelids match head color  
7. Tail is always present  
8. Tail color = yellow  
9. All shapes are rounded  
10. Components do not touch (except tail-body)  

---

## 🎬 16. MOTION PRINCIPLES

- Float, never grounded
- Tail leads expression with slight delay
- Body follows with gentle ease
- Use smooth easing curves
- Avoid rigid motion

---

## 🧠 17. STYLE DNA (SUMMARY)

> A floating alien built from soft rounded volumes, with oversized eyes, a front-mounted mouth block, a bottom-heavy head silhouette, a spherical body, pink mitten hands, and a yellow whip-like spiral tail that drives expressive motion.

---

## 🎯 18. USAGE CONTEXT

### Works for:
- UI mascots
- Voice assistants
- Educational apps (Miotomo)
- Animation loops
- Branding systems

---

## 🔥 19. EXTENSIBILITY

You can vary:
- Eye direction
- Mouth shape
- Head proportions (slightly)
- Animation behavior

You must NOT vary:
- Structure
- Color system
- Tail type
- Material style

---

## ✅ FINAL CHECKLIST

Before approving any new character:

- [ ] Eyes are large and aligned  
- [ ] Eyelids match head color  
- [ ] Mouth is front-mounted  
- [ ] Head wider at bottom  
- [ ] Hands are solid pink  
- [ ] Body is floating sphere  
- [ ] Tail is yellow spiral  
- [ ] Proper spacing between parts  
- [ ] No extra details added
